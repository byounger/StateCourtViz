OVERVIEW

Inspired by the National Center of State Courts existing court diagram pdfs, I wanted to primarily focus my visualization on the process of searching and learning about a case’s appeal process. The visualization is meant to be exploratory at first and gradually become an explanatory snapshot of a specific appeal process. Each step a user takes, gets he or she closer to understanding a state’s court system, what type of cases and what type of courts that are associated with a selected state.

First Part of the Visualization:

Users can look up states through the search navigation bar at the top of the map, select states based on certain population groups and compare.

Second Part of the Visualization:

Users can review and learn about a selected state’s court structure and possible cases.

APPROACH

Based on the timeline of the developing the visualization and existing data structure, I decided to build my visualization using Tableau Public. It is a great tool that allowed me to connect and sync specific tables, add quick detailed tooltips and create unique displays.
